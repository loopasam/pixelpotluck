ingredients:
  - 2 roasted eggplants flesh
  - 1/4 cup tahini
  - 1 lemon
  - 1 garlic
  - 1 tablespoon Greek yoghourt
  - 1/4 teaspoon salt
  - pepper
  - 1 teaspoon sumac
  - 1 teaspoon red pepper flake, optional
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil

steps:
  - mix everything
  - serve with pita bread
