Baba Ganoush Baba Ganoush

A delicious dip made from eggplant

Ingredients

  • 2 roasted eggplants flesh
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Greek yoghourt
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sumac
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flake, optional
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. mix everything
  2. serve with pita bread
