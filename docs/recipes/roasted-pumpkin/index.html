Roasted Pumpkin

The sweet and earthy flavors of autumn as a base for soups, baked goods, and more.

⏳ Planning needed: Yes

🍽️ Servings: Enough material for a few recipes

🧺 Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized pumpkin 🎃
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil 🏺

🗒️ Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven 200°C (400°F).
  2. Cut the pumpkin 🎃 in half and scoop out the seeds and pulp. Leave the skin on.
  3. Spread the olive oil 🏺 on the pumpkin halves 🎃 and place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Roast for 25-30 minutes or until the pumpkin 🎃 is tender and edges are slightly browned. Time will vary depending on the size of the pumpkin.
  5. Let cool for at least 10 minutes. Scoop out the flesh and discard the skin.
  6. Use immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week. Keeps well in the freezer.

✏️ Notes

  • You can also roast the seeds for a crunchy snack, just add salt and pepper to taste.
