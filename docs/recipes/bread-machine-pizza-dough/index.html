Bread Machine Pizza Dough

This recipe guides you through making an effortless, foolproof pizza dough using a bread machine. It's perfect for pizza lovers who want to enjoy homemade pizza with minimal effort.

⏳ Planning needed: No

🍽️ Servings: Enough dough for one large pizza

🧺 Ingredients

  • 390 grams bread flour 🌾
  • 7 grams active dry yeast 🍞
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil 🏺
  • 1 cup warm water 💧
  • 2 teaspoons sea salt (if your flour mix doesn't contain any) 🧂

🗒️ Instructions

  1. Place all the ingredients in a bread machine (🌾, 🍞, 🏺, 💧, 🧂).
  2. Start the dough cycle and let the machine do the work.

✏️ Notes

  • Spread on a backing plate lightly covered with fine cornmeal, in order to improve crispness.
  • Add your favorite toppings and bake at 230°C (450°F) for 15-20 minutes.
