Pancakes

Perfect for a lazy Sunday breakfast or a quick weekday treat, these pancakes are sure to delight everyone at your table. Serve them hot with your favorite toppings for a breakfast that's both comforting and delicious.

⏳ Planning needed: No

🍽️ Servings: Makes about 8 to 10 pancakes

🧺 Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour 🌾
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder 🥄
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar 🍚
  • ¼ teaspoon salt 🧂
  • 1 ¼ cups milk 🥛
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted 🧈
  • 1 egg 🥚

🗒️ Instructions

  1. Whisk together all the dry ingredients (🌾, 🥄, 🍚, 🧂).
  2. Add to the bowl all the wet ingredients (🥛, 🧈, 🥚).
  3. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately ¼ cup for each pancake.
  6. Brown on both sides (2-3 minutes) and serve hot.

✏️ Notes

  • Add your favorite sliced fruit to the batter for a fruity twist.
  • Add a dash of vanilla extract to the batter for a sweet twist.
  • Add a dash of cocoa powder or chocolate chunks to the batter for a chocolatey twist.
