Baba Ganoush

Baba Ganoush, a delightful Middle Eastern dip, is renowned for its creamy texture and smoky flavor.

Planning needed: Yes

Servings: A large bowl, enough for 4 people

Ingredients

  • 2 roasted eggplants flesh
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Greek yoghourt
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sumac
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flake, optional
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. mix everything
  2. serve with pita bread

Notes

  • You can keep it up to 2 weeks in the fridge
  • Delicious with pita bread
  • You can add some sumac for a more sour version
  • You can add some parsley on top
